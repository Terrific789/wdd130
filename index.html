Nigeria

Nigeria is a vibrant West African country rich in cultural heritage, with over 250 ethnic groups, including Hausa, Yoruba, and Igbo. Its diverse landscapes range from tropical forests to savannas, mountains, and coastal plains. Nigeria boasts the largest economy in Africa, driven by its abundant natural resources, including oil, natural gas, and agricultural products. Despite facing challenges like economic inequality, corruption, and security concerns, Nigeria is a hub of creativity, innovation, and resilience, with a thriving music scene, rich literary heritage, and a rapidly growing young population.
